18.12.2024
Christoph Spiegel (Zuse-Institut Berlin)
Coloring the plane with neural networks
Abstract: We present two novel six-colorings of the Euclidean plane that avoid monochromatic pairs of points at unit distance in five colors and monochromatic pairs at another specified distance \( d \) in the sixth color. Such colorings have previously been known to exist for \( 0.41 < \sqrt{2} - 1 \leq d \leq 1/\sqrt{5} < 0.45 \).
